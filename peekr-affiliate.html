Does the discount code track affiliates even without the link?
Yes. If a customer uses your promo code without clicking your affiliate link, the sale is still credited to your account   same as if they used the link.

Paid ads are not allowed. If you use paid advertising, your commissions will not be paid. We conduct regular checks to ensure compliance.
How does link tracking work?
Cookies and tracking codes monitor your referrals for 30 days. Each time someone clicks your link or uses your code, the 30-day window resets   so you always get credit.
Is there a limit to how much I can earn?
No limit whatsoever. The more you refer, the more you earn   forever. There's no cap on commissions.
