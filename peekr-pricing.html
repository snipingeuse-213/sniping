Questions & answers

Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, with no questions asked.
What counts as a "new store"? +
A new store is a Shopify store detected by our scanner for the first time. On the Starter plan, you see up to 500 new stores per day. On Pro and Agency, it's unlimited.
How is the data collected? +
Peekr pulls real data directly from our official platform partners including Shopify and Meta. This ensures accuracy and freshness you can't get from scrapers.
Can I switch plans later? +
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via Stripe. All transactions are secured and encrypted.
